This is a layout of a portable USB based temperature logger that can monitor the Temperature 
conditions of a small area. Specifically used for various cold chain management industries to 
monitor the temperature variations in critical transportation of medicines, Food etc. 
The device is battery operated and can log data upto 10000 readings. The device stores 
the reading and can be accessed with the help of USB interface.

Technology used : Digital Temperature Sensor, SPI, USB, RS485, Lithium Battery.
